HOW IT WORKS:
-------------
BEFORE:
  Login (08:00) → Start Location Time ❌
  Meeting 1 (09:30-11:00)
  Meeting 2 (12:00-14:00)
  Logout (17:00) → Out Location Time ❌
  
AFTER:
  Login (08:00)
  Meeting 1 (09:30-11:00) → Start Location Time ✅
  Meeting 2 (12:00-14:00) → Out Location Time ✅
  Logout (17:00)
